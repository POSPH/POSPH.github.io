Everything you need at the counter

Built for the everyday Filipino tindahan.

Designed for the way Philippine shops actually run — quick sales, mixed payments, Senior & PWD discounts, and full visibility on the day's takings.

Fast point of sale

Scan, type, or pick — every line takes under a second. Keyboard shortcuts for the regulars: F12 checkout, F4 Senior/PWD, F1 new sale.

VAT & Senior/PWD math

The math nobody wants to redo by hand. VAT-inclusive shelf pricing, then a clean Senior & PWD 20% discount with the VAT exemption applied correctly.

Phone as a scanner

Use any Android phone as a Bluetooth-free wireless barcode scanner — connects over your shop Wi-Fi. PRO

Cashiers with roles

Each sale is tagged to the cashier on shift. Admin sees the full picture; cashiers only see their own till. PRO

Receipts & barcode labels

Prints to any 58/80 mm thermal printer or A4 office printer. Generate barcode sticker sheets for fresh stock in two clicks.

Stock & daily totals

Low-stock badges, movements log, and live "sales today" KPIs the moment a sale closes. No spreadsheets to maintain.

Dashboard
